About MBA At ABBS

ABBS MBA is the first program in the world to combine business, entrepreneurship and technology. With an ABBS MBA, you'll explore entrepreneurship in a global, collaborative and multidisciplinary context through our online MBA program. You'll graduate with the skills, knowledge and connections needed to become a competitive entrepreneur.

  • Hone skills such as leadership, financial management, or marketing while learning the latest developments in business.
  • Learn the basics through to advanced topics created by business leaders and world-class professors.
  • Set yourself up to make an impact on tomorrow's organizations with a credential that will be recognized for years to come.

Curated from experts--MBA programme at ABBS offers courses that are academically sound and highly engaging.
